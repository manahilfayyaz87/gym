Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your membership with 30 days' written notice. We offer a 7-day free trial so you can experience the gym before committing.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?

No contracts required. We offer flexible month-to-month memberships. We believe in earning your business every month through excellent service.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely! You can change your membership plan anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

Is there a sign-up fee?

No sign-up fees! We occasionally run promotions where we waive the first month, so ask about current offers when you visit.

Are personal training sessions included?

Basic sessions are included with Standard and Premium memberships. Additional sessions can be purchased separately starting at $50/session.

Can I bring a guest?

Yes! Basic members can bring guests for a small fee. Standard and Premium members get complimentary guest passes.

What if I have questions about my fitness plan?

Our expert trainers are available to answer all your questions and create personalized fitness plans. Book a consultation with any of our staff.

Do you offer student or corporate discounts?

Yes! We offer 15% discount for students and 20% discount for corporate groups (5+ members). Contact us for more details.
