About IRON FORGE

Empowering fitness enthusiasts since 2015

Our Story

IRON FORGE was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to create a gym that puts people first. We started as a small fitness facility with just a handful of dedicated members and two passionate trainers. What began in a 2,000 sq ft space has grown into a state-of-the-art 15,000 sq ft facility serving over 2,000 active members.

Our founder, Michael Peterson, envisioned a space where people of all fitness levels could come together to achieve their goals. He believed that with the right equipment, expert guidance, and a supportive community, anyone could transform their body and their life.

Today, IRON FORGE stands as a beacon of excellence in the fitness industry. We continue to invest in the latest equipment, training methodologies, and member services to ensure our community thrives.
